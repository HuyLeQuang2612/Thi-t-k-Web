One purticular book which is a recommended reading is The Street Lawyer by John Grisham. This book is about a lawyer who begins reevaluating his priorities in life when a bad incident occurs within his law firm. Consequently, he become acquainted with the inner city streets, and realize the harsh existence of the homeless, and vows to give them a chance in the courts. The Street Lawyer is a great book it is a well written and interesting. Other book by John Grisham in clude The Firm, The Pelican Brief, and The Client
